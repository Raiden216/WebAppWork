Basic Information
I am an aspiring game developer in Pakistan. Ever since I was introduced to video games
as a child, they have been a big part of my life. These pockets of alternate realities telling all these
amazing stories. As I grew up, my interest has only become deeper and my curiosity
led me to further learning how they work. How they're made, what thought processes
go into their design, and eventually what I need to do to learn to make my own.

My love for video games has led me to study programming deeply as a result.
As such, I am well-versed in languages such as Python, C++ and Java, and aim to
learn even more. As a passionate programmer, I navigate through the intricate pathways
of technology with creativity and precision, crafting digital experiences that transcend expectations.
Join me on a journey where every keystroke propels us closer to the future of innovation.
