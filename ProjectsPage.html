Projects

Here is a collection of projects that I've worked on in the past.

Connect 5

A small python-based game that was made as a variation of the classic 'Connect 4' game. It was made with customisable colour themes, a menu system and the variant rule of needing to join 5 pieces in a row rather than 4.

Treasure Taker

An item collection game created in Unreal Engine 5. The player has to navigate a small area and collect randomly generated collectibles, while avoiding the patrolling enemies that aim to capture them. The layout is themed like a pyramid in the desert, and has some secrets to discover for those who like exploration.

Hunted in a Maze

An escape game set in a maze, where the player must find their way through a labyrinth and find the way out. The maze is litered with power-ups for the player to use while trying to stay away from the Hunter, who is tracking them down to capture them. The Hunter is quite intelligent, and aims to cut off the player's path towards the exit in the most effective ways, so players must be clever to avoid him.
